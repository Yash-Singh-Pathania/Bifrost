# Bifrost — Logo & Brand Brief

## What is Bifrost?

A desktop app for **semantic video search**. Import a video, Bifrost indexes the audio transcript and visual content, then search by meaning — not keywords — and jump to any moment.

## Name Origin

Bifrost is the **rainbow bridge** in Norse mythology — connecting worlds. The app bridges the gap between you and the exact moment you're looking for inside a video.

## Design Context

- **UI style:** Dark glassmorphism (visionOS-inspired — frosted glass panels, subtle blur, spatial floating elements)
- **Current palette:** `#0A0A0A` background, white text, `rgba(255,255,255,0.12)` borders, semi-transparent glass surfaces
- **Typography:** SF Pro (system font), clean and minimal
- **Platform:** macOS, Windows, Linux (Electron)

## Logo Direction

### Ideas to explore

1. **Abstract bridge / rainbow arc** — a minimal geometric arc or path connecting two points
2. **Prism / light refraction** — light entering and splitting into searchable moments
3. **Lettermark "B"** — sharp, geometric, works as app icon and favicon
4. **Search + video hybrid** — play button or film frame + search lens, abstracted

### Constraints

- Must work at **16x16px** (favicon), **512x512px** (app icon), and large (website)
- Must look great on **dark backgrounds**
- Needs to work as a **macOS app icon** (rounded square / squircle mask)
- Simple enough to be recognizable at small sizes

## Color Direction

The app currently uses white-on-dark with no accent. The logo is the chance to define one.

### Options

- **Rainbow gradient** (subtle) — ties into the Bifrost name
- **Single accent** — cool blue `#0A84FF`, warm amber, or prismatic
- **Iridescent** — a subtle shimmer effect for the logo mark

Whatever color is chosen becomes the app's accent throughout the UI.
